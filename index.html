The Legacy of Formula One

Formula One is the pinnacle of motorsport, Ever since the 1950's it was considered the most prestigious motorsport outweighing others such as Nascar and MotoGP. It has evolved throughout the years in terms of its technology, safety, and overall popularity. There are many reasons for people to love this sport, one of them being the natural competitiveness and enjoyment you sense by just watching these drivers go at it on the track, Although Formula One and its racing styles put the drivers at risk due to the dangerous tactics and stunts that they do while trying to overtake each other it makes it all the more fun for the spectators and gives you a huge adrenaline rush. There are many names that have been written in legend as the best Formula One drivers in its history, the most prominent name would be Michael Schumacher. Schumacher was known for being one of the most decorated drivers of his sport, winning 5 consecutive championships and leading his team to win 6 consecutive constructors championships. Another driver would be the recently knighted Sir Lewis Hamilton, who is still racing to this day with 7 drivers championship titles and he holds the records for the most wins and podium finish marking him one of the best Formula One drivers ever

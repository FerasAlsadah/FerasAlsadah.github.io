Welcome to the world of Football

Football, which is also known as soccer in various places around the world, is the most popular sport ever known gaining a global following of 4.0 billion people, other sports don't come as close to football in global following as the second most popular sport would be cricket with 2.5 billion global following

The aim of football is to score more goals then your opponent in a 90 minute playing time frame. The match is split up into two halves of 45 minutes. After the first 45 minutes players will take a 15 minute rest period called half time. The second 45 minutes will resume and any time deemed fit to be added on by the referee (injury time) will be accordingly.

Each team consists of eleven players. These are composed of one goalkeeper and ten outfield players. The dimensions of the pitch vary from ground to ground, but are roughly 120 yards long and 75 yards wide. On every pitch, you will have a 6-yard box next to the goal mouth, an 18-yard box surrounding the 6-yard box and a center circle. Each half of the pitch must be the same in terms of dimensions.

the equipment that is needed for a soccer match is a playing field and a football ball. Players can be found wearing studded football boots, shin pads and matching strip. Additionally players can be found wearing studded football boots, shin pads and matching strips. Goalkeepers will wear padded gloves because they are the only ones allowed to handle the ball. Each team will have a player who is responsible for leading the team.
